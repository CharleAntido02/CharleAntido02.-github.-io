Home

Minecraft is a sandbox video game first made by Markus "Notch" Persson. It was sold to Microsoft in 2014 for 2.5 billion American dollars. Over 238 million copies of the game have been sold. This makes it the best-selling video game of all time.

It is a game made up of blocks, creatures, and community. You can survive the night or build a work of art; the choice is all yours.

Minecraft have a great number of mobs, passive and hostile.Passive mobs don't attack you, but some passive mobs attack back when you provoke them such as wolf, llama.

However, Hostile mobs are aggressive they will attack you when they see you, like zombie, skeleton and creeper.
